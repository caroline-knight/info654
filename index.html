What makes a good sky picture?

  • Bold colors, especially orange, red, and purple
  • Reflections on water
  • Sentimental value/positive memories

The following table shows my top 16 favorite pictures of sunrises and sunsets ranked. Each row contains the ranking, the image, the location, and the year the picture was taken. My top 5 also include descriptions with additional background information.
