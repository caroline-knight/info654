Not-So-Great Sky Pictures

What makes a not-so-great sunset picture?

  • Uninteresting view
  • Unrealistically high expectations
  • Too many or too few clouds
  • Lack of:
    • Bold/bright colors
    • High contrast
    • Good visibility
  • Poor camera quality
